Welcome to the FAQ section of "Engineering Words: My Journey Through English 101." Here, you'll find answers and insights that reflect my experiences in academic writing, tailored for future English 101 students seeking guidance and inspiration in their writing journey.


A: In my AI Ethics Case Study Analysis project, I engaged with the ethical implications of AI use in classrooms, specifically analyzing the case of a student who inadvertently used AI in their presentation. This task deepened my understanding of the subject, sharpened my problem-solving skills, and challenged my critical thinking and analytical abilities. It also aligned with the course's Rhetorical Awareness and Critical Thinking goals, as I considered multiple perspectives to propose well-reasoned solutions. As stated in my analysis, “The professor must decide between upholding the class rules and the fabric of academic integrity regardless of the student’s intent... or using the situation to help their students understand the importance of performing due diligence on the tools they use by checking to see if they use AI” (Zeller, 2023, p. 3.). The skills I gained in analyzing information and offering solutions can be applied to future ethical and analytical writing tasks, enhancing my ability to handle complex real-world situations.
