Frequently Asked Questions For English 101


Welcome to the FAQ section of "Engineering Words: My Journey Through English 101." Here, you'll find answers and insights that reflect my experiences in academic writing, tailored for future English 101 students seeking guidance and inspiration in their writing journey.


A: In the AI Ethics Case Study Analysis project, I engaged with the ethical implications of AI use in classrooms, specifically analyzing the case of a student who inadvertently used AI in their presentation. This task deepened my understanding of the subject, sharpened my problem-solving skills, and challenged my critical thinking and analytical abilities. It also aligned with the course's Rhetorical Awareness and Critical Thinking goals, as I considered multiple perspectives to propose well-reasoned solutions. As stated in my analysis, “The professor must decide between upholding the class rules and the fabric of academic integrity regardless of the student’s intent... or using the situation to help their students understand the importance of performing due diligence on the tools they use by checking to see if they use AI” (Zeller, 2023, p. 3.). The skills I gained in analyzing information and offering solutions can be applied to future ethical and analytical writing tasks, enhancing my ability to handle complex real-world situations.

A: In the AI Policy project for English 101, I focused on reflecting on my writing process and assessing my strengths and weaknesses. This activity aligned with the reflection goal of the course, enabling me to critically evaluate my work and learning process. The project involved drafting a policy to “protect academic integrity within the University of Arizona by providing detailed guidelines on the ethical and responsible use of generative AI in the Fall 2023 ENGL 101-426 class” (Zeller, 2023, p. 1). It underscored the importance of distinguishing between AI-assisted and original work, emphasizing transparency and proper citation. This reflective process helped me assess how my values and circumstances informed my writing choices and highlighted the need to adhere to academic integrity. Additionally, it allowed me to apply these insights to future writing projects, fostering a deeper understanding of my writing journey and its broader implications in academic contexts.

It allowed me to reflect on the emotional aspects of my academic journey, fostering a deeper understanding of literacy’s impact on individual perceptions. Furthermore, the skills I gained in digital storytelling and literacy reflection through this project can be applied to future academic and personal endeavors, contributing to my ongoing learning journey.
