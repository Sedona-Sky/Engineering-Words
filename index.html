Welcome to Engineering Words: My Journey Through English 101, where I, Sedona Sky Zeller, blend my love for engineering with the art of writing. This website will guide and inspire future English 101 students and demonstrate how effective communication is crucial in every aspect of our lives, even in fields like engineering.

In the FAQ section, I reflect on my academic journey, from analyzing ethical dimensions of AI in case studies and fiction to creating digital literacy narratives. Each question and answer demonstrates the depth of my learning journey. These projects allowed me to articulate intricate ideas in AI policy, evolve my storytelling through digital formats, critically assess TED Talks, and present complex topics in engaging infographics. My experiences have sharpened my writing skills and deepened my grasp of narrative's role in critical thinking.

Beyond academics, I'm a person of diverse interests. From immersing myself in the imaginative worlds of science fiction, like Andy Weir's "Project Hail Mary," to expressing my creativity through art, both on paper and digitally, I enjoy exploring various facets of creativity. For example, baking and scuba diving are my go-to activities for tranquility, while traveling and spending quality time with my family are the moments I cherish the most.

Looking ahead, I aim to become an optical engineer, where I can collaborate on innovative solutions for grand challenges. I want to contribute to humanity's survival and advancement on Earth and in the cosmos. This website is not just a collection of writing tips and insights; it's a window into how I intertwine my academic, personal, and future professional aspirations. Join me on this site as I share insights and tips for excelling in English 101, all while weaving in elements of my journey and aspirations.
